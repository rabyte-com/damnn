About Valorant

Valorant is a team-based tactical first-person shooter developed by Riot Games. Players choose from a range of unique agents, each with their own abilities that complement team strategies. The game emphasizes strategy, team coordination, and skillful shooting.

Meet the Agents

Phoenix

A duelist who uses fire-based abilities to heal and control space, Phoenix is a self-sufficient agent.

  • Abilities: Fireball, Wall of Fire, Phoenix's Resurrection
  • Role: Duelist

Sage

A healer and support agent, Sage can block entrances, heal teammates, and even revive them in battle.

  • Abilities: Healing Orb, Barrier Orb, Resurrection
  • Role: Controller

Jett

Jett is an agile duelist who can dash around the map, making her one of the most mobile agents in the game.

  • Abilities: Tailwind, Updraft, Cloudburst
  • Role: Duelist
